ABOUT US

Getting to know us better

ORGANIZATION CHARTER

The Chinese Australian League is a professional organization which has members who are lawyers, doctors, architects, accountants, teachers, engineers and professionals from various other fields.

GOALS

Our aim is to provide assistance and advice to our members through the cooperation or its participating individuals. This is to be achieved by promoting understanding between the citizens and companies of Australia and China.

It is also our aim to assist individuals and companies from China and Australia communicate and cooperate in various fields including:

  • Technology
  • Culture assimilation and understanding
  • Economics
  • The integration of the these two great cultures

THEME

To work positively toward happiness and contentment in life, and assist it’s members to adapt to the changes and challenges in their various endeavours.

To assist our members with:

  • Language
  • Culture
  • Work Skills
  • Social understanding

FUTURE

Establish the Chinese Australian League as a dynamic force within the two communities and to promote a reliable platform for professionals and other individuals to meet and laise in harmony.
